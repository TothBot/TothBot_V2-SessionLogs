missions, subsequent sessions (TB00151c through
TB00151k = 8 sessions) kept the alpha-suffix pattern
instead of incrementing to TB00152, TB00153, etc. Each
of TB00151c..k was a STANDALONE session with its own

original split.

self-detection or Bill notification. The convention

TB00151j) remain as-is in GitHub and disk history —
