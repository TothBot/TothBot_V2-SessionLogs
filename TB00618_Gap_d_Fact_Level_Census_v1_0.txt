2. WHY GAP-1 IS A GENUINE DD-014 GAP (NO-GUESS)
============================================================
  rule:SS-WIRE-001 is present in the live diagram ONLY as a bound token
  in the D7 mod:Startup_Sequence constraints list (dv1_182 L8108). The
  ACTIONABLE wiring a coder needs to avoid DEFECT-SS-009 -- "the regime
  engine is seeded at construction with a single-pair default universe
  because the 50-pair universe is built later by WS_Manager from the
  instrument snapshot, so the daily/00:00-UTC regime computation must be
  driven with the current WS_Manager universe, not the construction-time
  universe" -- is NOT stated in any visible <text> or <desc> field. The
  token monitored_universe appears ZERO times in dv1_182 (grep-verified);
  the live "monitored universe" prose strings (BTC/USD anchor, AR-074,
  regime-computation-regardless-of-N) are about universe membership and
  regime scope, NOT the construction-timing/data-flow ordering. From the
  diagram alone a coder would construct the regime engine and call its
  daily computation with its construction-time universe -> regime
  computed for BTC/USD only -> Gate-3 blocks all non-BTC entries (the
  exact DEFECT-SS-009 loss-of-opportunity failure). LOSS-RELEVANT: a
  silent single-pair regime collapse degrades the whole pipeline. This
  is the one construction/wiring fact in 1011014/1011002 not buildable
  from dv1_182.
