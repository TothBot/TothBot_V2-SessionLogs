THE MISSION (Bill, at the TB00794 close). "I have heard a lot about Bitget.
Things have changed a lot since I started this. Research what are the best
exchanges for TothBot_V2 with the MOST trading options."

THE GATING CONSTRAINT. TothBot's operator is a US PERSON. Score "most trading
options" AGAINST "legally plus practically usable by a US person, with an
automated-trading API and a sandbox." Never recommend a venue a US person cannot
lawfully use.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE HEADLINE ANSWER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The venues with the MOST trading options (Bitget, Binance, Bybit, OKX) are
precisely the ones a US person CANNOT legally use. Once the hard filter is
applied, the field collapses to a short list and the INCUMBENT (Kraken) wins it
outright. The biggest 2026 change: CFTC-regulated US PERPETUAL futures are now
real, and Kraken bought the best perps stack in the country (Bitnomial, Payward
acquisition closed 2026-05-01; US-retail perps launched 2026-06-15 on the
Bitnomial exchange via Kraken Pro). The incumbent did not just stay competitive,
it absorbed the very venue the TB00794 carry-forward had identified as the perps
path. The "direct Bitnomial vs Kraken-Pro middleman" question is now MOOT, they
are one corporate stack.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BILL'S NAMED VENUE - BITGET
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Genuinely huge: ~1,300+ spot coins, ~660+ perpetual pairs, up to 125x,
best-in-class copy-trading, clean HMAC plus passphrase API with a demo flag. AND
NONE of it is legally available to a US person. No US entity, no US license,
bitget.com geo-blocks US IPs, the US is an explicitly restricted jurisdiction in
its ToS, VPN access breaches the ToS and risks account/fund freeze. The only
US-reachable Bitget product is its self-custody Web3 wallet, not the exchange.
Maximum options, ZERO US-legal access. Off the table.

RECOMMENDATION FOR TOTHBOT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SPOT (today): STAY ON KRAKEN. Confirmed the best-available US-legal home base.
   Nothing beats it on options x US-access x cost as a single venue: spot + 10x
   US spot-margin + 9-coin CFTC perps + CME dated futures = the widest US-legal
   breadth anywhere, with a mature REST+WS API the organism already speaks
   (HMAC-SHA512, native stop/trailing types) plus a real Futures demo sandbox.
   Switching venues would buy nothing and cost the existing integration.

2. PERPS EXPANSION (the validated TB00794 WHAT): the best US-legal perps venue is
   KRAKEN DERIVATIVES US / BITNOMIAL.
   - The cheap perp fee the TB00794 probe proved is the load-bearing lever for
     reviving shorts is exactly Bitnomial's flat ~$0.07-0.10/contract, confirming
     the verdict economics with a primary source.
   - Two access modes, same matching engine: Kraken Pro (unified cross-collateral
     wallet, simplest, reuses Kraken auth) OR direct Bitnomial FCM (own
     REST/WS/FIX/DMA, more control, isolated derivatives organism). Which one is a
     subsequent diagram-first HOW.
   - ONE real engineering constraint surfaced: Bitnomial is LIMIT-ORDERS-ONLY (no
     native market/stop/stop-limit). TothBot's L2/L3 stop + emergency-SL logic
     must be SYNTHESIZED CLIENT-SIDE. The sacred 1:1.5 is unaffected (it sets exit
     prices, placed as limits), but this is a genuine design item for the perps
     slice.

3. COINBASE is the credible #2 perps venue, but only 4 coins, perpetual-style
   ~5-yr-dated (not true perps), static sandbox. Keep as fallback, not primary.

4. Everything offshore is unusable, and the onshore spot-only venues (Binance.US
   at 0.02% taker, OKX US, Robinhood) add nothing TothBot needs that Kraken does
   not already provide, while lacking the perps that are the actual expansion goal.

NET: the research set out to find whether anything BEATS the incumbent on
options x US-access x cost. Nothing does. The incumbent has, since the spot arc,
