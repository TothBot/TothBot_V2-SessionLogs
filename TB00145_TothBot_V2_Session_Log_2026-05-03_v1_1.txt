6. PAPER-vs-LIVE PERMITTED DIVERGENCE POINTS (the ONLY
   ones permitted; per TB00000 v2_18 §4.4 PA-004)
============================================================

The following four divergence points are physically
unavoidable. Live MUST differ from paper at exactly
these points and nowhere else:

(1) PRIVATE WEBSOCKET CONNECTION.
    Paper: does NOT connect to Kraken private WS.
    Live:  connects to Kraken private WS.
    Why:   Paper does not place real orders. Connecting
           private WS in paper would create an
           authenticated session that exists for no
           operational purpose. Paper testing was
           performed without private WS — that is the
           validated configuration.

(2) ORDER DISPATCH.
    Paper: simulates orders locally; no outbound order
           messages are transmitted to Kraken.
    Live:  transmits orders to Kraken via private WS.
    Why:   Paper trading by definition does not move
           real money. Live trading by definition does.

(3) CAPITAL LEDGER.
    Paper: synthetic spot_usd_balance starting at
           paper_starting_balance ($1,000 per D-05);
           updated by simulated-fill P&L applied
           locally using the SAME fee schedule
           (FEE_MAKER_PCT, FEE_TAKER_PCT) Kraken
           applies in live.
    Live:  real Kraken spot_usd_balance from
           snap_orders / balances events.
    Why:   No real capital moves in paper. A synthetic
           ledger is required to give CIATS a P&L
           signal. Synthetic ledger uses identical fee
           arithmetic to live so paper P&L is shape-
           equivalent to live P&L.

(4) POSITION MIRROR SOURCING.
    Paper: populated EXCLUSIVELY by the local
           simulated-fill path.
    Live:  populated by Kraken executions events and
           snap_orders reconciliation.
    Why:   No real Kraken position state exists for
           paper-mode TothBot strategies. Real Kraken
           positions held by Bill outside TothBot must
           not contaminate paper-trading state.

OUTSIDE THESE FOUR POINTS, paper and live MUST be
