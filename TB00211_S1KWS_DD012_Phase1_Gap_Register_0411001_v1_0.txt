2.1 NATURE OF 0411001 - WIRE-CONTRACT SPECIFICATION.
0411001 is fundamentally distinct from AA=08 and AA=10
specs previously audited under DEC-012(a-extended)
methodology. AA=08 and AA=10 specs are TOTHBOT
ARCHITECTURE specifications - they encode TothBot
internal architectural decisions (gates, layers, regimes,
modules) that are canonical in 0500000 diagrams.

0411001 is the AUTHORITATIVE WIRE CONTRACT between
TothBot and the Kraken WebSocket v2 interface. Its
content is overwhelmingly KRAKEN-DEFINED EXTERNAL FACTS
that TothBot must conform to:
  - WebSocket endpoint URLs (Kraken-defined).
  - JSON message schemas for all channels (Kraken-
    defined wire format).
  - Authentication protocol (Kraken-required REST
    GetWebSocketsToken sequence).
  - Channel subscription formats (Kraken-required JSON
    parameter shapes).
  - Wire-protocol pitfalls (Kraken-imposed: underscore
    vs hyphen stp_type form, tick_size deprecation,
    edit_order deprecation/prohibition).
  - Rate limit numerics (Kraken-imposed: 2.34/sec
    decay, 80 orders per pair, maxratecount tier-
    dependent).
  - Python library configuration (websockets asyncio
    client max_size, open_timeout, max_queue,
    ping_interval).

These facts are NOT TothBot architectural decisions.
They are external constraints. 0500000 diagrams encode
TothBot architecture and do NOT encode (and should not
encode) Kraken's wire protocol surface. The relationship
between 0411001 and 0500000 is OPPOSITE the relationship
between AA=08/AA=10 specs and 0500000:
  - AA=08/AA=10: subordinate spec restates 0500000
    architecture (bucket (a) candidate).
  - 0411001: subordinate spec documents external
    constraints; 0500000 references those constraints
    via canonical AR rules that the wire contract
    implements.
