audit finding. The clarified mechanic (confirmed this session): the
take-profit open order is the Exit Controller's resting closing sell on
an already-owned Filled Position; on a partial TP fill the Filled
Position splits into a sold part and a still-owned smaller Filled
Position; TP_PARTIAL_FILL_REMAINDER is the exit_reason for the DUST case
only -- when that smaller Filled Position is below Kraken qty_min/cost_min
so no valid resting protective order can sit on it, the Exit Controller
force-closes it at market. (Long side; short side is the AR-073
direction-symmetric mirror -- close via reduce_only market buy.) This is
DISTINCT from the D2/G3b ENTRY-side partial fill, where the unfilled
ENTRY open order is CANCELLED with no Filled Position to sell.
