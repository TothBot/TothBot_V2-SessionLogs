2. BILL'S STRUCTURE STRATEGY - BACKTEST  [scripts/tb00777_structure_strategy.py, Code b009d13]
   Daily, ~2yr, 32 pairs. 1R = entry->stop distance (the stop just beyond the last daily resistance, ABOVE
   for shorts / BELOW for longs); position sized to 1R so a full stop = exactly -1R (pair-size-neutral, R
   sums cleanly). Structural trail (rolling extreme, ratcheted in our favor; tighter window after scale).
   Bank HALF at +3R (= +1.5R locked). Runner to EMA/MA reversal or the tighter trail. Conservative intrabar
   (adverse before favorable). Nested IS(0-0.5)/OOS(0.5-1.0) + per-direction split + 3-way OOS sub-window
   stability + fees (taker x2 + short open) + per-day short rollover, all in R.
   RESULT (5 scale/exit configs x 4 fast signals = 20 cells): the structure HITS 1.5-2.7 R:R EVERY time
   (as designed) but EVERY cell is net-NEGATIVE (ER -0.03 to -0.43R). win 22-34% < ~37% break-even; 55-91%
   full-stopped pre-scale. CONCLUSION: exits are validated; the GAP is ENTRY QUALITY.
