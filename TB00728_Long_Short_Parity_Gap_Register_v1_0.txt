1. DECISIONS NEEDED FROM BILL (the only two; everything else is AUTO)
============================================================

   DECISION A - SHORT MARGIN BORROW / ROLLOVER FEE.
     Shorts run on Kraken MARGIN (ar:AR-009). A margin short pays an opening margin fee +
     a rollover fee (per ~4h held) that a spot LONG never pays. This cost feeds short net
     P&L and therefore the SACRED net 1:1.5 R:R floor and the paper synthetic ledger
     (PA-005 paper=live parity). It is in NO figure and NO registry seed today.
     -> Bill must rule: model it now (as a CIATS-owned seed, starting value = Kraken's
        published margin open + rollover rates, refined from paper - the DEC-124 seed-then-
        correct pattern), OR defer it (build short without the borrow leg, flag for pre-live).
     Affects: Image1 FEE block, Image9 compute:net_loss, sec-12.4 ledger, Gate-8 R:R,
     Image7 PA-004(3). NEW SEED if modeled: margin_open_fee_pct + margin_rollover_fee_pct.

   DECISION B - SHORT PARTICIPATION IN THE NON-DIRECTIONAL REGIMES.
     The six-regime grid: TRENDING_POS -> Long; TRENDING_NEG -> Short; NON_DIR_ELEVATED ->
     both blocked (whipsaw, symmetric - no decision). The open cell is NON_DIR_NORMAL
     (regime 3): today the code/taxonomy permits LONG at half-size (0.5) and does NOT permit
     short. Symmetric alternative = Short also trades NON_DIR_NORMAL at half-size.
     -> Bill must rule: (i) Short trades confirmed downtrends ONLY (TRENDING_NEG), leaving
        NON_DIR_NORMAL long-only (current code); OR (ii) symmetric - Short also trades
        NON_DIR_NORMAL at half-size (mirror of Long). No new numeric value either way (0.5
        already seeded); this is a strategy/risk policy choice only Bill can make.
     Affects: Image4 regime-3 cell, gate:G3/G4/G6, sec-5 prose, taxonomy.py PROFILES.
