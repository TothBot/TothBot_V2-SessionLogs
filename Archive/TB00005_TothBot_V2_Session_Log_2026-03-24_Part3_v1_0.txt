on fill confirmed correct architecture. Bill confirmed:
PL-011 CLOSED permanently. Rejection reason documented
in AR-008 of 0500000 dv1_4.

3. COMPLETE CHANGE RECORD — dv1_4 CHANGES TO 0500000
============================================================

Seven changes applied to 0500000 in this session:

C-1 — MPP Always Enforced on Market Orders
  Market Price Protection (MPP) is now always enforced
  on Kraken market orders — the no_mpp override flag
  is deprecated and ignored. Risk documented for:
  - Layer 2 market sell (may reject on wide spread
    during fast adverse moves — exactly when Layer 2
    fires most frequently). Exit Controller MUST
    handle MPP rejection and retry. Assign to 0511004.
  - Layer 3 emergSL trigger (triggered market order
    subject to MPP on wide spread). Assign to 0411001.
  Added to: Section 4.1 table (Layer 2 and Layer 3 rows),
  Section 11.2, Section 11.3, Diagram 1, Diagram 3,
  Diagram 6.

A-1 — AR-001 Expanded with Explicit Rejection Reasons
  AR-001 now documents explicit evaluated-and-rejected
  analysis of both:
  (1) cancel_all_orders_after (WS v2 DMS): would cancel
      ALL orders on TothBot failure including protective
      off-book emergSL stop orders. Defeats crash
      protection it is supposed to support.
  (2) FIX API cancel-on-disconnect: same architectural
      conflict. Cancels all orders on session drop,
      destroying emergSL crash protection.
  Both evaluated and rejected on identical grounds.
  Added to: Section 8 AR-001 row, Section 2.1.

A-2 — cl_ord_id on All Outbound WS Messages
  All add_order and batch_add calls now carry cl_ord_id
  (client-assigned alphanumeric identifier, up to 18
  characters). TothBot assigns at dispatch time before
  Kraken response. Position Mirror records immediately
  at dispatch — no async gap. Cancel operations may
  reference cl_ord_id.
  Added to: Section 2.1, Section 2.4 (A-2 description),
  Section 8 AR-002, AR-006, Section 11.1,
  Diagram 1, Diagram 2, Diagram 6.
  Assign to: 0511001, 0511003, 0511005.

A-3 — WS snap_orders as Preferred Restart Reconciliation
  WS executions snap_orders:true provides full open
  orders snapshot over WS at zero REST call cost.
  Preferred over REST GetOpenOrders on restart.
  REST GetOpenOrders remains as cold-start fallback.
  Added to: Section 2.1, Section 2.4 (A-3 description),
  Section 8 AR-003, Section 11.1.
  Assign to: 0511001, 0511006, 0211001.

A-4 — stp_type:cancel-newest on All add_order Calls
  Self Trade Prevention added to all add_order and
  batch_add calls. Prevents inadvertent self-fills
  when a prior GTD entry has not expired before next
  signal fires for same pair.
  Added to: Section 2.1, Section 2.4 (A-4 description),
  Section 8 AR-006, Section 11.1,
  Diagram 1, Diagram 2, Diagram 6.
  Assign to: 0511003.

A-5 — Reconnect Constraint Distinction
  Two distinct reconnect behaviors now documented:
  (1) Random drop: reconnect instantly, handful of
      attempts
  (2) Confirmed maintenance: minimum 5-second delay
  Previously only the 5-second rule was documented.
  Added to: Section 2 constraints note (was in Section 2.4),
  Diagram 1 constraints box.
  Assign to: 0511001.

A-6 — OTO Conditional Orders: Evaluated and Rejected
  OTO conditional orders evaluated as alternative to
  batch_add for emergSL placement. Rejected because:
  (1) uses limit price not actual fill price — systematic
      adverse bias to sacred R:R;
  (2) creates Position Mirror race condition.
  batch_add confirmed correct.
  Added to: Section 4.1 (note in exit table), Section 4.2,
  Section 8 AR-008, Diagram 3, Diagram 6.
