DEFECT-WM-RECONNECT-001 — FIX SPECIFICATION
===========================================

Component:   ws_manager.py (dv1_19 — current)
Governing:   1011002_WS_Manager_Coding_Spec dv1_18
Severity:    CRITICAL — blocks stable paper trading

Problem Statement
-----------------

Transient WebSocket disconnects from Kraken's CloudFlare
proxy are propagating out of the asyncio TaskGroup inside
ws_manager.py, causing WS_MGR_FATAL and systemd restart.
Observed frequency: approximately 1-2 per hour.

Observed signatures (from 2026-04-16 to 2026-04-18):
  - ConnectionClosedError: no close frame received
    at _recv_loop_public:975
  - ConnectionClosedOK: received 1001 (going away)
    at _send_public:735 (via _ping_loop:2360)
  - ConnectionResetError [Errno 104]
    at _recv_loop_public:975
  - RuntimeError: Fatal reconnect failure
    at _initiate_reconnect:3241

Required Behavior
-----------------

ConnectionClosedError, ConnectionClosedOK, and
ConnectionResetError within public WebSocket loops must
be CAUGHT LOCALLY and trigger orderly reconnect without
process termination.

_initiate_reconnect must retry with backoff. It must
NOT raise RuntimeError on first failure. It propagates
fatal WS_MGR_FATAL only after all retry attempts are
exhausted.

Private WebSocket loops must be similarly hardened (symmetric
treatment).

Fix Scope
---------

  full 7-field header, revision history entry).

                 locally, trigger orderly reconnect path.
    Line 958   — _main_loop TaskGroup: ensure transient
                 errors from children do not bubble to
                 TaskGroup. TaskGroup only receives
                 genuinely fatal errors.
    Line 975   — _recv_loop_public: catch transient
                 disconnect locally, trigger orderly
                 reconnect path.
