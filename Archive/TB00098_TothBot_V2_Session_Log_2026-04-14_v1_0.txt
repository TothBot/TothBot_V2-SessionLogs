  CHANGE 1 — New method _rest_build_pair_maps():
    Inserted immediately before _warm_up_all_pairs().
    Calls GET /0/public/AssetPairs (public, no auth).
    Uses self._make_http_session() per established pattern.
    For each pair in result:
      Normalizes wsname: XBT/ → BTC/, /XBT → /BTC.
      Populates _wsname_to_classic and _classic_to_wsname.

      _classic_to_wsname for reverse lookup.

      pair_cache, uses that.

    Logs PAIR_MAP_BUILD_FAILED(reason=str) on error.
    Never raises.
