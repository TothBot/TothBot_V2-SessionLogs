      either mode. Caller contract per 0500000
      dv1_23 §12.3.

      entry_timeout_sec        : GTD parameter for
                                 entry orders (30-60s

                                 owned).

                                 (20 starting;

      All read at evaluation time, not cached.

      only.
      Execution Engine MUST NOT directly mutate
      position_mirror. All writes route through
      WSManager helper methods. HR-PM-009 read-
      only-via-helpers compliance.

  (e) Cross-module interfaces
      EE -> WSManager:    add_order, batch_add,
                          cancel_order (seam
                          methods).
      EE -> Risk Engine:  position_size_request,
                          drawdown_check.

                          ENTRY_ACCEPTED,

                          ENTRY_CANCELED_NO_FILL,
                          ENTRY_CANCELED_PARTIAL_FILL.
      EE inputs:          Signal Pipeline Gate 8
                          dict, executions channel
                          callback dispatch.

  (f) AR-006/-007/-008/-018/-032/-033/-046/-047
      binding.
      All entry / TP / emergSL message construction
      delegates to WSManager. EE constructs the
      semantic intent (symbol, qty, price, R:R
      target, ATR-derived stops); WSManager

      Kraken WS v2 messages.
