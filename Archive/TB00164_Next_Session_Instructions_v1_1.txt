§4.1  TB00000 v2_22 -> v2_23 .txt
      NEW §4.5 — DIAGRAMS AS CANONICAL DESIGN
      DOCUMENTS — ABSOLUTE STANDING RULE.

      Pattern: §4.4 PA-001..010 enforcement model.
      Rules to author (DD = Design Document):

      DD-001  DIAGRAMS ARE THE CANONICAL DESIGN
              DOCUMENTS. Diagrams in 0500000 (and
              future Tier-2-and-below diagram-bearing
              specs) are the AUTHORITATIVE source for
              all architectural rules, contracts,
              decisions, and constraints they encode.
              Prose contextualizes; it does NOT
              govern.

      DD-002  DIAGRAM-OVER-PROSE AUTHORITY HIERARCHY.
              When prose and diagram conflict in the
              same controlled document, the diagram
              governs. Prose is updated to match the
              diagram, NOT the reverse.

      DD-003  THE 4-STEP WORKFLOW (per TB00152 SL
              §5.2). Specifications proceed in this
              order:
                Step 1 — Complete the diagrams
                         (Design Documents) — alter,
                         add, remove
                Step 2 — Update 0500000 prose to
                         match the diagrams
                Step 3 — Write/rewrite the 10XXXXX
                         coding specs to match
                Step 4 — Code from documents that
                         make sense
              Steps proceed in order. Step N MAY NOT
              begin until Step N-1 is complete.

      DD-004  NO PROSE-FIRST AUTHORING. New
              architectural rules, contracts,
              decisions, or constraints MUST first be
              encoded in the appropriate diagram(s)
              (root <desc> governs: list, labeled-
              group <desc>, or visual content). Prose
              authoring follows diagram authoring.
              Authoring rules without a corresponding
              diagram element is a defect.

      DD-005  CONFLICT-DETECTION VALIDATION.
              At every controlled-document
              authoring/amendment cycle, the diagram
              and prose are cross-checked. Discrepancies
              are tracked in the Master Defect
              Register and resolved by updating prose
              to match diagram, never the reverse.

      DD-006  RECURRENCE TRACKING. Every DD-* rule
              violation is logged in the current
              session's Session Log under "Claude
              Errors This Session" with rule cited,
              defect described verbatim, and
              corrective taken. NSI files carry the
              recurrence count forward.

      DD-007  PRE-EMIT SCAN. Before authoring or
              modifying any controlled document
              prose, Claude verifies the prose
              content does not introduce
              architectural rules / contracts /
              decisions / constraints absent from
              the corresponding diagram. Self-catch
              on violation:

                DD self-caught — prose-first
                authoring detected — redrafting.

      DD-008  RATIONALE. From TB00152 SL §5.1
              verbatim Bill quote: "Claude does not
              have the ability to keep all of the
              connections and gates straight using
              multiple and multiple pros
              requirements... [diagrams] have become
              our 'Design Documents'... The diagrams
              control everything and we proceed from
              the diagrams." Confirmed by Bill in
              TB00163 chat-turn 4: "Diagrams need
              to be completed and become governing
              as canonical-over-prose... it creates
              fewer documents to control drift than
              following 30 documents with pros.
              Flaws JUMP out using the diagram
              method." First-principles drift
              detection: prose-only is O(N²) in
              document count; tagged-diagram-indexed
              is O(N).

      DD-009  RELATIONSHIP TO §15.13 HTML CANONICAL
              FORMAT. §15.13 (0311001) governs the
              FILE FORMAT of diagram-bearing
              controlled documents (single .html
              file). §4.5 (TB00000) governs the
              AUTHORITY HIERARCHY between diagram and
              prose WITHIN that file. The two rules
              are complementary, not redundant.

      DD-010  SUPERSESSION OF PRIOR LESSON-TEXT.
              Any prior session-log lesson text
              about diagram-vs-prose authority is
              SUPERSEDED by §4.5. Lesson-text-only
              governance is insufficient (per OI-037
              six-recurrence precedent). §4.5 is
              absolute and overrides any contrary
              instruction in any prior session log,
              NSI, or governance document.
