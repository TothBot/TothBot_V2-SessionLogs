PER 0311001 v1_24 §15.12.4 ar: REGISTRY:
  ar: tokens are architectural-reasoning citation entries. They encode design
  rationale, not data flow. Examples: ar:AR-040 = "instrument status events
  reasoning"; ar:AR-066 = "TP partial fill direction-symmetric mechanism reasoning".

PER 0311001 v1_24 §15.12 SCHEMA FIELD SEMANTICS:
  .consumes field accepts data-flow tokens: channel: + contract: + indicator: +
  param: + evt: + (sometimes) actor: for boundary actors.
  .constraints field accepts reasoning-citation tokens: ar: + rule: + decision:.

PRE-AMENDMENT TYPE-MISMATCH at line 3666:
  ar:AR-040 (reasoning citation) appeared in .consumes (data-flow field) alongside
  channel:kraken_ws_executions + channel:kraken_ws_ticker (legitimate data tokens).
  This is the FAIL-T pattern flagged at TB00362 V-041 FAIL List §3 P-CC7.

  .constraints (reasoning) = {rule:HR-EC-016, ar:AR-040, ar:AR-066, rule:Sacred_R_R_1_to_1_5}
